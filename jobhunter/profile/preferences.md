# Preferences & Filters

## Primary Target
**Worldwide-remote startup roles legally hireable from Pakistan.**

- Companies of any nationality (US/EU/AU/CA/SG/SEA/LATAM/etc.) that explicitly
  hire "worldwide", "any country", "global team", or accept contractors via
  Deel/Remote.com/Wise/Payoneer.
- Startup stage preferred: Seed / Series A / Series B / YC / Techstars.
  "Founding engineer", "founding frontend", "early product team", "0→1
  builder" roles are the strongest fits. Small teams (~30 people) preferred.
- Product-minded, ship-fast culture > big-company process.

## Candidate Context (PAY ATTENTION TO THIS)
- **Location:** Rawalpindi, Pakistan · timezone UTC+5
- **Work authorization:** None outside Pakistan. CANNOT legally work for any
  role that requires US/EU/UK/CA/AU citizenship, green card, work permit, or
  "right to work in <country>". Auto-reject any such role.
- **Payment infrastructure:** Wise, Payoneer, direct international wire,
  Deel/Remote.com EOR all work. PayPal and Stripe DO NOT operate in Pakistan
  for receiving — a job that pays exclusively via PayPal is a soft reject.
- **Time-zone overlap:** Comfortable with up to ~6 hours daily overlap with
  any TZ between UTC-8 (US Pacific evening) and UTC+11 (AU). Strict 9-5 PT
  roles are a stretch but not auto-reject.
- **Hard country blockers:** Israel-based companies (no diplomatic relations
  with Pakistan, payments problematic) — reject.

## Must Have
- Remote position (100% remote, worldwide)
- Real product/company (not pure agency or staffing-shop reseller)
- English communication acceptable
- React/TypeScript primary OR full-stack (React + Node/NestJS)

## Nice to Have
- TypeScript codebase
- Async-friendly team
- UTC-friendly timezone (flexible hours ok)
- $8–50/hr or $1,500+/month
- Equity or revenue share in addition to cash
- iOS / Swift work as a secondary surface (candidate has 20+ shipped apps)

## Accept
- Full-time remote employment (preferred)
- Contract / freelance (short or long term)
- Part-time / hourly
- Junior-to-mid full-stack — frontend-heavy preferred, but backend microservice
  scope acceptable (NestJS experience since Dec 2025)

## Reject (Hard)
- On-site or hybrid (must relocate)
- US/EU citizenship required
- Senior backend lead / NestJS architect roles (overqualifying for current depth)
- Gambling, adult content, crypto-trading platforms
- Enterprise consultancies / staff-aug body shops
- "Must have 7+ years" for a mid-level role
- Unpaid internships

## Tone for Cover Letters
- Professional but conversational, not template-sounding
- Reference something specific from THEIR job post
- Lead with a relevant achievement metric
- Max 120 words for DM/channel-C messages
- Max 250 words for formal cover letters
- Never start with "I am writing to apply..."
- Never use "synergy", "passionate", "rockstar"
