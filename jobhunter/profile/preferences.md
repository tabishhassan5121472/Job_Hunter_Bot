# Preferences & Filters

## Primary Target
**Startup companies** — Seed / Series A / Series B / YC / Techstars-backed.
"Founding engineer", "founding frontend", "early product team", "0→1 builder"
roles are the strongest fits. Small teams (under ~30 people) preferred.
Product-minded, ship-fast culture > big-company process.

## Must Have
- Remote position (100% remote, worldwide)
- Real product/company (not pure agency or staffing-shop reseller)
- English communication acceptable
- React/TypeScript primary OR full-stack (React + Node/NestJS)

## Nice to Have
- TypeScript codebase
- Async-friendly team
- UTC-friendly timezone (flexible hours ok)
- $8–50/hr or $1,500+/month
- Equity or revenue share in addition to cash
- iOS / Swift work as a secondary surface (candidate has 20+ shipped apps)

## Accept
- Full-time remote employment (preferred)
- Contract / freelance (short or long term)
- Part-time / hourly
- Junior-to-mid full-stack — frontend-heavy preferred, but backend microservice
  scope acceptable (NestJS experience since Dec 2025)

## Reject (Hard)
- On-site or hybrid (must relocate)
- US/EU citizenship required
- Senior backend lead / NestJS architect roles (overqualifying for current depth)
- Gambling, adult content, crypto-trading platforms
- Enterprise consultancies / staff-aug body shops
- "Must have 7+ years" for a mid-level role
- Unpaid internships

## Tone for Cover Letters
- Professional but conversational, not template-sounding
- Reference something specific from THEIR job post
- Lead with a relevant achievement metric
- Max 120 words for DM/channel-C messages
- Max 250 words for formal cover letters
- Never start with "I am writing to apply..."
- Never use "synergy", "passionate", "rockstar"
